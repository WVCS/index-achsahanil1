Members

Sungjin

His full name is Park Sung-Jin (박성진). He is mostly known by Sungjin. He is the Leader, Main Vocalist, and Guitarist. He was born on January 16, 1993. He was born in Busan, South Korea.

Jae

His full name in Park Jae-hyung (박재형). He is mostly known by Jae, Jae Park or eaJ. He's the main guitarist, lead vocalist and rapper. He was born on September 15, 1992. He was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina and grew up in Long Beach, California.

Young K

Hi full name is Kang Young-hyun (강영현). He is mostly known by the stage names Young K and Brian Kang. He is the Band's singer, bassist and is also an acotr. He was born on December 19, 1993. He was born in Goyang, Gyeonggi Province, South Korea.

Wonpil

His full name is Kim Won-pil (김원필). He is mostly known by Wonpil. He is the band's vocalist, keyboardist and synthesizer. He was born on April 28, 1994. He was born in Incheon, South Korea.

Dowoon

His full name is Yoon Do-woon (윤도운). He is mostly known by Dowoon. He is the drummer, sub-vocalist and the maknae of Day 6. He was born on August 25, 1995. He was born in Busan, South Korea

Who?

DAY6 (also stylized Day6; Korean: 데이식스, Japanese: デイシックス) is an all male South Korean band formed and managed by JYP Entertainment in 2013. The band made their debut on September 7, 2015 in Korea and currently consists of 5 members.

Where?

They have preformed all over the world and have applied to a large audience. Their Current location and "home base" are located in the capital of South Korea. They are part of JYP Entertainment and Practice at the main building.

Why?

They are a Kind and have written music that covers many different Genres. They want to be a voice for Young adults and voice the feelings in their own minds and hearts.

Other

On March 8, Sungjin announced that he has enlisted to the military for his mandatory military service in the same day.

On August 6, it was reported that Young K, Wonpil and Dowoon would be forming the band's first sub-unit with their debut being scheduled for this month. On August 11, it was revealed through an image teaser that the unit will be debuting on August 31 and was named Even of Day.
